Comprehensive Healthcare
Support Services

From medical consultations to mental wellness — we cover the full spectrum of community health needs.

🩺

Medical Consultation

Access qualified doctors and specialists through our partner network of 12 clinics and 3 hospitals across Hyderabad.

  • General physician consultations
  • Specialist referrals
  • Follow-up care coordination
Request access →
💊

Medication Assistance

We help patients who cannot afford essential medicines by coordinating with pharmacies and donor networks.

  • Chronic disease medication support
  • Post-surgery prescription aid
  • Monthly medicine delivery
Apply now →
🧠

Mental Health Support

Licensed counselors and psychologists available for free sessions, especially for low-income households.

  • 1-on-1 counseling sessions
  • Group therapy programs
  • Crisis helpline (7 days)
Get help →
🚗

Transport Assistance

We arrange free or subsidised transport to clinics for elderly, disabled, and economically vulnerable patients.

  • Ambulance coordination
  • Auto/cab scheduling
  • Wheelchair-accessible vehicles
Book transport →
🏠

Home Care Services

Trained home-care aides and nursing volunteers visit patients who cannot travel for treatment.

  • Post-op recovery care
  • Elder care support
  • Wound dressing & vitals monitoring
Schedule a visit →
💰

Financial Assistance

We connect patients with government schemes, NGO grants, and donor-funded subsidy programmes.

  • Arogyasri & Aarogyam linking
  • Emergency medical fund
  • Hospital bill negotiation
Check eligibility →

Our Mission

Why CareConnect?

Built specifically for under-resourced communities that need healthcare access the most.

🩺

Patient-First Approach

Simple, compassionate support pathways without medical jargon. Every patient is assigned a personal care coordinator.

🤖

AI-Powered Assistant

Instant answers to common queries, freeing care teams to focus on complex, critical cases that need human attention.

📊

Smart Coordination

Submission data is categorised and routed intelligently, giving coordinators instant visibility into what is needed.

🌐

Multilingual

Our team and AI assistant support Telugu, Hindi, Urdu, and English — serving Hyderabad's diverse communities.

🔒

Privacy & Trust

All patient data is stored securely and never sold or shared with third parties. Your trust is our foundation.

🤝

Community Driven

Owned and operated by the community. Our volunteers, advisors, and donors are all local to Hyderabad.
